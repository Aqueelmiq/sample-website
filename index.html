Urban Farming

About Us

Created in the Fall of 2018, the new Urban Farms (UF) EPICS team work with Peace Garden and Farms to help improve the availability of fresh produce through urban agriculture. Peace Garden and Farms is aiming to build capacity to grow foods locally in a USDA designated food desert in northwest Indiana.

The first project the team will take on is to design a rooftop garden at the Peace Farms facility, which is located at a former National Guard Armory in Gary, Indiana. The project will include work to understand the needs of the community, assess the existing structure to determine load limits of the garden, and the design and construction of the gardens themselves. Consider contributing your talents, while learning a diverse set of skills, to this important new project.
